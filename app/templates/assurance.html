▸ Methodology (for the AI/ML engineer)

Trap set: {{ assurance.n_traps }} adversarial + {{ assurance.n_clean }} clean, from 4 mutation operators over the verified claims (number_drift · unsupported_superlative · false_equivalence · true_but_unsayable).

Gate decision: rules (veto — can only restrict) → retrieve (BM25) → NLI entailment → diverse ensemble. entail_raw = min(mean(support lenses), min(veto lenses)) — numeric & superlative are veto lenses, coverage + NLI are support; GREEN ≥ 0.60, AMBER ≥ 0.30, else RED; final = most_severe(entailment, rule).

Baseline: a single number-blind coverage judge (token overlap), caught iff similarity < 0.5 — the deliberately-gameable single-LLM-judge stand-in.

Calibration: isotonic regression (pool-adjacent-violators) fit on (raw confidence, is-sayable); ECE over 5 bins of the calibrated probabilities.

All deterministic + offline. The full per-claim trace (BM25 scores, NLI prob, ensemble votes, calibrated confidence) is in the golden tables below and the Observatory ledger.
