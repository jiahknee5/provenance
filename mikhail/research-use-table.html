What Mikhail's Research Can Usefully Add to Provenance

PM-aligned readout: keep our work focused on front-end intake, the claim ledger, evidence review, and safe initial data collection. Enrichment is useful only after it becomes a cited Provenance claim.

Core framing

We are not building a dossier engine. We are building the front end and intake layer for a provenance ledger.

Reusable primitive

Every external signal becomes a cited, reviewable claim before it influences copy, recommendations, or scoring.

Near-term lane

Claim ledger UI, evidence inspection, review queue, email preview, and optional import review.

Working rule: external enrichment should never bypass Provenance. It enters as evidence, becomes a cited claim, passes review/Gate policy, and only then can it affect generated copy or recommendations.
