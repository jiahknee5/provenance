Fixing the risk2 / 2

We bound the optimizer to only-true messages — so it can't win by lying.

An optimizer told to maximize replies normally learns to exaggerate (Goodhart / reward-hacking). We make that structurally impossible.

⚷
Constrained self-optimization: a variant only enters the A/B arena after it passes verification. A winning lie can't be discovered, because lies never compete. The guardrail is the product.
