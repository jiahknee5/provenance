2 · The Gate · Catalyst & partners08

Catalyst & hiring partner gating

Catalyst claims verified
✓ Employee E is SWE / PM / designer at company Y
✓ Y enrolls E in engineering or cross-functional squad
✓ Y has scope + buy-in for real AI capstone
✓ Format match: engineering vs sales & CX vs custom

Rules differ: employer handles immigration; eligibility = role + company + program config.

Hiring partner claims verified
✓ C meets H’s min criteria: stack, location, seniority, comp
✓ No conflicting contractual terms
✓ Role reqs: shipped agents / RAG at Gauntlet
✓ Partner JD ↔ challenger performance evidence

All stored in ledger — marketing promises about candidates backed by program data.

3 · Optimizer09

Constrained bandit — routing qualified humans

Actions only enter the pool after Gate clearance. Rewards = program outcomes, not vanity metrics.

CONTEXT FEATURES
Individuals: yrs, stacks, domains, prefs (founding vs IC, location, comp, Prime willingness)
Companies: industry, headcount, AI maturity, Catalyst history, hiring velocity, Prime vs train-in-place preference
ACTIONS (GATE-CONSTRAINED)
Candidates: Prime interview now · defer cohort · partner pool only · suggest Catalyst at employer
Companies: pitch Catalyst (eng / sales & CX) · hiring-partner credits · both

Never routes to a program the candidate can’t legally or structurally join — structural fix for Goodhart.
