Situation

Consumers expect Netflix-level relevance everywhere. B2B buyers are no different — they ignore generic outreach. Personalization is the game.

Complication

In regulated industries, every specific sentence — in an email, on a landing page, in an ad — is a legal claim. Legal can't review 100,000 unique variants. So you ship one safe, generic experience — and wonder why conversion flatlines.

Resolution

Provenance sits in front of every channel. Every personalized touchpoint is auto-checked and pre-cleared before it ships — email, web page, or ad copy. Launch same day. Each visitor gets their own verified experience.

What marketers actually want

Not "Dear {FirstName}." Not one homepage for everyone. Real personalization — on every channel.

Imagine every touchpoint built around this specific person or account — their size, industry, regulatory context, ROI profile. That's what converts. AI can generate it. The blocker was always: who verifies 100,000 unique variants before they ship?
