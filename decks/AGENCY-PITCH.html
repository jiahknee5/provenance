Ultra-personalization breaks human review. We automate the review — so 1:1 at scale becomes your edge, not your liability.

You can't legally read 100,000 unique messages. So today, "personalize everything" forces a choice: generic-and-safe, or personal-and-risky. We dissolve that choice — every message is personalized and provably true and compliant, automatically.

Situation

1:1 personalization is now the standard. Buyers ignore the generic; the winning message is the one written for them — their data, their situation, their objection.

Complication

At true 1:1 scale, no human can review every message. In any claims-heavy business, that means you either dumb it down to one reviewed template, or you ship unverified claims and invite a fine.

Resolution

Move verification inside the work. Automated, per-claim checking + truth-bounded optimization make ultra-personalization safe by construction — at any volume.
